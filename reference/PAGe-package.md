# PAGe: Seasonal Flu Curve Alignment and Forecasting

Prospective one- and two-week-ahead forecasts of seasonal
respiratory-virus positivity. A gated pipeline detects epidemic
ignition, aligns partial seasons to historical reference curves, and
produces binomial generalized additive model forecasts with adaptive
bias correction. Training and evaluation support leakage-safe
leave-one-season-out validation, prior-informed tuning, candidate
racing, and explicit promotion gates.
